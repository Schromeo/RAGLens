# Legacy Refund Policy 2021

This archived policy is retained for historical reference.

Physical products can only be returned within 14 days of the purchase date.

Customers must include the original receipt and all original packaging. Returns without original packaging may be rejected.

Refunds are processed within 15 to 20 business days after warehouse inspection.

This document should not be used for current customer support decisions unless a case explicitly refers to an order from the 2021 policy period.

# Refund Policy

Customers may return most physical products within 30 days of the purchase date.

To qualify for a refund, the product must be unused, undamaged, and returned with its original packaging when possible. Refunds are issued to the original payment method after the returned item has been inspected.

Refunds are usually processed within 5 to 10 business days after the returned item is received by the warehouse.

Digital gift cards, downloadable software, and final sale items are non-refundable unless required by law.

For security reasons, customers may be asked to verify their email address before account changes or refund requests are processed.

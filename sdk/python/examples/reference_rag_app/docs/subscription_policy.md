# Subscription Policy

Customers may cancel a subscription at any time from the account billing page.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Customers usually keep access to paid features until the current billing cycle ends.

Subscription charges are generally non-refundable once the billing cycle has started, unless required by law or approved by support for a documented billing error.

Changing from an annual plan to a monthly plan takes effect at the next renewal date.

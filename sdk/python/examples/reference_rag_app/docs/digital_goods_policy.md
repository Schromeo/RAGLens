# Digital Goods Policy

Downloadable software, license keys, digital gift cards, and other digital goods are generally non-refundable after purchase.

A refund may be considered only when the digital item was not delivered, the license key was invalid, or a duplicate charge occurred.

Customers who purchased downloadable software by mistake should contact support, but support is not required to issue a refund unless an exception applies.

Digital gift cards cannot be exchanged for cash unless required by law.

# Legacy Refund Policy

This legacy policy applies only to older internal support documentation.

Physical products can only be returned within 14 days of the purchase date.

Returned products must be unused and include the original packaging. Refunds may be denied if the product shows signs of damage, misuse, or missing accessories.

Refunds are reviewed by the support team before being issued.

This document may conflict with the current public refund policy and should not be used for customer-facing decisions.

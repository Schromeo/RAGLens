# Account Policy

Customers can reset their password using the email address associated with their account.

Account deletion requests are usually completed within 14 days. Once an account is deleted, order history and saved preferences may no longer be recoverable.

Customers may update their name, password, and default shipping address from the account settings page.

For security reasons, customers may be asked to verify their email address before account changes are processed.

If a customer loses access to their email address, they should contact support for identity verification.

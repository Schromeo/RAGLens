# Shipping Policy

Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days after an order has been processed.

Expedited shipping usually takes 2 business days after an order has been processed.

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. Customers receive a tracking number by email once the order has shipped.

Shipping delays may occur during holidays, severe weather, or carrier disruptions.

For security reasons, customers may be asked to verify their email address before account changes or shipping address updates are processed.
